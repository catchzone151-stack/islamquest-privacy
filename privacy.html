IslamQuest – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2025

IslamQuest respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the IslamQuest mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name and avatar
  • App progress, streaks, levels, and quiz results
  • Basic usage and device data for app stability

We do not collect:

  • Real names
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Payment information

2. How We Use Information

Data is used only to operate the app, save progress, and improve reliability.

3. Data Storage

Data is securely stored using third-party services such as Supabase. Reasonable measures are taken to protect user data.

4. Data Sharing

IslamQuest does not sell or rent personal data. Data is shared only when required for app functionality or legal compliance.

5. Children’s Privacy

If a child has provided personal data without consent, contact us and the data will be removed.

6. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion at any time. Data will be removed where technically possible.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance.

8. Contact
