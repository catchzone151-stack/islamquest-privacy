IslamQuest - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

IslamQuest is an educational application designed to help users learn Islam through lessons, quizzes, and guided content. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

Free Users

No personal information is required. No login is required. All progress is stored locally on the device.

Premium Users

Users who purchase premium may create a username and password. This is used to restore purchases and sync progress across devices.

2. Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store any payment card information.

3. Data Storage and Security

Premium account information is encrypted and stored securely. Free users' data remains local to the device.

4. Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for ages 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Billing
  • Capacitor / Ionic framework services

No advertising or tracking SDKs are used.

6. User Rights

Users may delete their premium account or request data deletion by contacting us at the email below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here.
